Our Story

5 IT Students Building a Streetwear Revolution Since 2025

Who We Are

NESTAR GAZE started as a passion project by five IT students from Cebu who shared a love for streetwear and technology. What began as late-night design sessions in our dorm rooms has evolved into a full-fledged e-commerce brand that bridges the gap between tech innovation and urban fashion.

As IT students, we bring a unique perspective to the streetwear industry—combining our technical expertise with creative design to build not just clothing, but a complete digital experience. Every piece we create is designed with purpose, crafted with quality, and inspired by the vibrant energy of Cebu's urban culture.

Our mission is simple: deliver premium streetwear that empowers young creatives to express themselves authentically, while building a brand that showcases what Filipino IT students can achieve when passion meets innovation.

Our Values

Tech-Driven

As IT students, we leverage technology in everything we do—from our website development to inventory management, ensuring a seamless shopping experience.

Student Community

Built by students, for students. We understand the lifestyle, budget, and style preferences of young creatives because we are you.

Innovation

We constantly experiment with new designs and technologies, bringing fresh perspectives from the intersection of IT and fashion.

Filipino Pride

Proudly made and designed in Cebu, Philippines. We celebrate our culture and showcase Filipino creativity to the world.

Affordability

Quality streetwear shouldn't break the bank. We price our products fairly because we know the student struggle firsthand.

Hustle Culture

We embody the student hustle—balancing studies, passion projects, and building a business. Our brand represents that grind mentality.

Our Journey

JAN
2025

The Idea

Five IT students in Cebu brainstormed a crazy idea: What if we combined our coding skills with our love for streetwear? NESTAR GAZE was born during our web development class project.

FEB
2025

Building the Platform

Countless all-nighters coding and designing. We built this entire e-commerce platform from scratch, learning as we go. Every bug fixed was a victory.

First Collection

We launched our first collection with just 10 pieces. Our friends and classmates became our first customers. Seeing people actually wear our designs was surreal.

One of our hoodies got featured on social media. Orders started pouring in from students across the Philippines. We were managing orders between classes!
